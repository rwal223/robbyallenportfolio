Graphic Design

HomeFirst for Kentucky Veterans commissioned my team to design a rehabilitation village for homeless veterans in Hazard, Kentucky. The village provides "soul care" therapy through communal living and, most significantly, gardening. Gardening feeds the village and establishes a sense of establishment and purpose.

Residences are attached to a greenhouse which hold vertical carousel planters.

Meanwhile, a community center acts to engage the village with outsiders, and holds recreational facilities.
